Exponential population growth model

In time Δt the population consisting of y elements grows by α⋅y elements: Δy=α⋅y⋅Δt, that is ΔyΔt=α⋅y.

With Δt→0 we get dydt=α⋅y, i.e. y′=α⋅y.

The initial population is y(0)=s.

The red line shows the exact solution of the differential equation y(t)=s⋅eαt. The blue line is the simulation with Δt=0.1.
